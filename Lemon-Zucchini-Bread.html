Lemon Zucchini Bread

This zucchini bread is delicious, light, and not overly sweet.

Lemon zucchini bread resembles a muffin and contains a hint of cinnamon and sweetness. As in this recipe, it's frequently broken up with delicate walnut pieces. You hardly even notice the taste of the zucchini because it is so mild. However, don't you find the tiny green flecks to be both fascinating and beautiful?

Ingreedients

  • 1½ cups shredded zucchini
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons lemon zest
