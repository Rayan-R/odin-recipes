Easy 3-Ingredient Pina Colada

In addition to being the simplest pia colada ever, it tastes so much better when made with real ingredients rather than pre-made mixes.

Pina colada or strained pineapple as it means in english is a is a rum-based cocktail that includes pineapple juice, coconut milk, and is typically served shaken or mixed with ice. Additionally the drink can serve as a fantastic dairy-free cocktail substitute.

Ingreedients

  • 2 cups chopped fresh pineapple
  • 1 (7 ounce) can sweetened condensed coconut milk
  • 7 fluid ounces white rum
  • 2 cups ice

Steps

  1. Combine pineapple, coconut milk, rum, and ice in a blender and puree until smooth and creamy. Serve immediately.
